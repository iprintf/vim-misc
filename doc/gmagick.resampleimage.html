Gmagick::resampleimage (PECL gmagick >= Unknown) Gmagick::resampleimage — Resample image to desired resolution 说明 public Gmagick Gmagick::resampleimage ( float $xResolution , float $yResolution , int $filter , float $blur ) Resample image to desired resolution. 参数 xResolution The new image x resolution. yResolution The new image y resolution. filter Image filter to use. blur The blur factor where larger than 1 is blurry, smaller than 1 is sharp. 返回值 The Gmagick object on success 错误／异常 错误时抛出 GmagickException。
