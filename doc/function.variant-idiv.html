variant_idiv (PHP 5, PHP 7) variant_idiv — Converts variants to integers and then returns the result from dividing them 说明 mixed variant_idiv ( mixed $left , mixed $right ) Converts left and right to integer values, and then performs integer division. 参数 left The left operand. right The right operand.
