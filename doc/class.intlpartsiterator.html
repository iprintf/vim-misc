The IntlPartsIterator class (No version information available, might only be in Git) 简介 Objects of this class can be obtained from IntlBreakIterator objects. While the break iterators provide a sequence of boundary positions when iterated, IntlPartsIterator objects provide, as a convenience, the text fragments comprehended between two successive boundaries. The keys may represent the offset of the left boundary, right boundary, or they may just the sequence of non-negative integers.
