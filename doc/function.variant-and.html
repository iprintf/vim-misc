variant_and (PHP 5, PHP 7) variant_and — Performs a bitwise AND operation between two variants 说明 mixed variant_and ( mixed $left , mixed $right ) Performs a bitwise AND operation. Note that this is slightly different from a regular AND operation. 参数 left The left operand. right The right operand.
