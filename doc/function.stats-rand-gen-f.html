stats_rand_gen_f (PECL stats >= 1.0.0) stats_rand_gen_f — Generates a random deviate 说明 float stats_rand_gen_f ( float $dfn , float $dfd ) Generates a random deviate from the F (variance ratio) distribution with "dfn" degrees of freedom in the numerator and "dfd" degrees of freedom in the denominator. Method : directly generates ratio of chisquare variates. 参数 dfn dfd 返回值
