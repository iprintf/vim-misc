Gmagick::solarizeimage (PECL gmagick >= Unknown) Gmagick::solarizeimage — Applies a solarizing effect to the image 说明 public Gmagick Gmagick::solarizeimage ( int $threshold ) Applies a special effect to the image, similar to the effect achieved in a photo darkroom by selectively exposing areas of photo sensitive paper to light. Threshold ranges from 0 to QuantumRange and is a measure of the extent of the solarization. 参数 threshold Define the extent of the solarization. 返回值 The Gmagick object on success. 错误／异常 错误时抛出 GmagickException。
