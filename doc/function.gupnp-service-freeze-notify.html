gupnp_service_freeze_notify (PECL gupnp >= 0.1.0) gupnp_service_freeze_notify — Freeze new notifications 说明 bool gupnp_service_freeze_notify ( resource $service ) Causes new notifications to be queued up until gupnp_service_thaw_notify() is called. 参数 service A service identifier. 返回值 成功时返回 TRUE，或者在失败时返回 FALSE。 参见 • gupnp_service_notify() - Notifies listening clients • gupnp_service_thaw_notify() - Sends out any pending notifications and stops queuing of new ones.
